## 這個 Skill 在做什麼

Removes named AI writing tells (tricolons, em-dash pileups, hedging stacks, sycophancy openers, stock vocab like "delve"/"crucial"). Split lint/rewrite modes 用於 auditing your own text 搭配out auto-rewriting. Five intensity levels, MIT。這筆資料來自 `awesome-agent-skills` 的索引清單，原始專案是 `MohamedAbdallah-14/unslop`。
